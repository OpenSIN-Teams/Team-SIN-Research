# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior: welcoming, respectful, constructive criticism, community-focused.
Unacceptable behavior: harassment, trolling, publishing private information, sexualized content.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers enforce these standards.

## Scope

Applies in all community spaces and when representing the community publicly.
